ENB Presets

For a very long time, I used Enhanced Vanilla ENB, which I used exclusively for over 400 videos. Enhanced Vanilla ENB stays true to Skyrim's original lighting, but gives you the option to add in nice ENB related effects, such as depth of field, godrays, detailed shadows, shadow fixes, Ambient Occlusion (SSAO), bloom, etc.

However, if you are looking for a performanc friendly ENB that makes Skyrim more fantastical - try out RealLike ENB v0113. I just started using this ENB preset and love it. Color saturation is up, daylight conditions look like daylight, stormy weather looks great, and nights can be bright, dark, or light depending on your choice. Here is a video of RealENB in action.

If those two options don't interest you, take a look atHodilton's channel. Hodilton makes great videos of ENB Presets, so browse throughout the ENB videos to find one that you like.
