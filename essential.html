ENB Presets

For a very long time, I used Enhanced Vanilla ENB, which I used exclusively for over 400 videos. Enhanced Vanilla ENB stays true to Skyrim's original lighting, but gives you the option to add in nice ENB related effects, such as depth of field, godrays, detailed shadows, shadow fixes, Ambient Occlusion (SSAO), bloom, etc.

However, if you are looking for a performance friendly ENB that makes Skyrim more "fantastical" - try out RealLike ENB v0113. I just started using this ENB preset and love it. Here is a video of RealENB in action. You may want to tone down the sun's brightness by editing the enbseries.ini file - find this line SunCoronaIntensity=6.0 and change it to SunCoronaIntensity=2.0.

If those two options don't interest you, check out Hodilton's YouTube channel. Hodilton makes great videos of ENB Presets, and other mods too, so browse throughout the ENB videos to find one that you like.
