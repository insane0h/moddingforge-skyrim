About/FAQ

About me!

ModdingForge-Skyrim is pretty much an overhaul of my previous r/skyrm modding posts. ModdingForge-Skyrim provides a better resource for those looking to mod Skyrim, and it's a bit easier to update and handle than reddit threads. I also wanted to learn responsive web design, so this was a great starting point.

Future goals of mine include similar resources for Fallout 4 (if it's ever released) and the next Elder Scrolls game.

I might also detail common mods for other games. Basically, I want to make moddingforge a resource for mods for many different games, not just Bethesda ones.

FAQ

Q: What do you record with?
A: FRAPS

Q: What mods do you use in your playthrough?
A: I've played Skyrim many times, and it differs on the playthrough. To set you up with the basic, essential, must have mods - check out this guide I made: http://www.reddit.com/r/skyrim/comments/1ikhrs/the_list_of_the_best_must_have_mods_for_skyrim/

Q: What ENB do you use?
A: 2012-2014 - Enhanced Vanilla ENB (v0113). Currently I use RealLike ENB v0113.

Q: Why the name "insane0hflex"?
A: It is a reference to Aqua Teen Hunger Force, a TV series I'm a big fan of (as you can see).

Q: What are your PC Specs and Framerate with Skyrim modded?
A: My current (as of October, 2014) PC specs are:

            CPU: Intel i7 2700k stock clocks
            GPU: nVidia GTX 560
            RAM: 8 GB DDR3
            HD: 1 TB 7200 RPM (no solid state drive yet!)
Average FPS: 45-60+ at 1080p. Note: I don't usually use full HD texture mods, because it causes dips into the low 20s when recording mod videos.
