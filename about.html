About/FAQ

About me!

ModdingForge-Skyrim provides a better resource for those looking to mod Skyrim, and it's a bit easier to update and handle than reddit threads. I also wanted to learn responsive web design, so this was a great starting point.

Future goals of mine include similar resources for Fallout 4 (if it's ever released) and the next Elder Scrolls game.

I might also detail common mods for other games. Basically, I want to make ModdingForge a resource for mods for many different games, not just Bethesda ones.

FAQ

Q: What do you record with?
A: FRAPS

Q: What ENB do you use?
A: 2012-2014 - Enhanced Vanilla ENB (v0113). Currently I use RealLike ENB v0113.

Q: Why the name "insane0hflex"?
A: It is a reference to Aqua Teen Hunger Force, a TV series I'm a big fan of (as you can see).

Q: What are your PC Specs and Framerate with Skyrim modded?
A: My current (as of October, 2014) PC specs are:

OS: Windows 7 Home Premium - 64bit
CPU: Intel i7 2700k - stock speed
GPU: nVidia GTX 560 - stock speed
RAM: 8 GB
Average FPS: 45-60+ at 1080p. I don't usually use full HD texture overhaul mods (Like Skyrim HD 2K), because it causes dips into the low 20s when recording mod videos. Also makes load screens really long since I don't have a SSD.
